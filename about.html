About

A ballot initiative is a form of direct democracy where the electorate petitions to put an issue up for a vote. This rarely happens in Canada.

As a journalist, I reported on four elections (2008 Canadian federal; 2007 and 2011 Ontario and 2006 Toronto). I also served as a deputy returning officer for the 2014 Ontario general election. Heading into this vote, I felt the most pressing issue was helping people fully understand Canada's electoral process in the easiest way possible before participating in it.
